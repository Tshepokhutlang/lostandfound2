Welcome to Lostings – Smart Lost & Found

Lostings is a unique online platform that reunites people with their lost belongings through a powerful matching system. Whether you've lost an item or found one, our tools make recovery fast, easy, and effective.

Step-By-Step Process

  1. Report an Item: Enter details like item name, category, photo, date, time, and location where it was lost or found.
  2. Submit Report: Review your entry and submit it securely to our system.
  3. Create Account: Receive an email with your dashboard login and account information.
  4. Print Fliers: Use your dashboard to generate and print custom fliers for local distribution.
  5. Get Matched: Receive real-time email notifications and system alerts when new items match your report.

Get Notified on Matches

Our smart matching system automatically compares lost and found reports. You’ll receive up-to-date email notifications and system alerts whenever a newly submitted item matches or closely resembles your lost or found entry.
